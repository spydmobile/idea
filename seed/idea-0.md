# [IDEA] Digital Social Media Safety Certification

## Summary
An open, multi-level, interview and test-based certification that gives any
person on Earth a verifiable credential proving they can navigate social media
safely and critically. Four levels: Aware (Seedling/Sprout/Navigator by age),
Informed, Resilient, Educator.

## Problem it solves
Digital literacy is assumed, never taught, and never verified. Platforms profit
from this gap. The result is mass manipulation, radicalization, election
interference, and mental health crises. No open, globally portable standard
exists for this skill. Countries like Australia are banning social media for
under-16s — this certification provides the readiness pathway that blunt
age-bans cannot: a capability gate instead of an age gate.
